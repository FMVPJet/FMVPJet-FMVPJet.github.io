I am Jiangtao Guo, currently working as a Algorithm Engineer for zxiat. Before that, I worked as a computer vision engineer for CASIVISION.
I was a Master of Engineering student in Guangdong Key Laboratory of Intellectual Property and Big Data (GDUT) supervised by Prof. Qingyun Dai.
