Jiangtao Guo (郭江涛)     

I am currently working as an Algorithm Engineer for iFlytek. Before that, I worked as an Algorithm Engineer at ZXIAT (Zhengzhou Xinda Institute of Advanded Technology) and CASIVISION.

I did my M.Eng. at Guangdong Key Laboratory of Intellectual Property and Big Data (GDUT) supervised by Prof. Qingyun Dai.

I am interested in deep learning and computer vision problems, especially 3D Object Recognition, Image Generation. My recent research topics include but not limited in Multi-modal Signal Recognition, UBSS (Underdetermined Blind Signal Separation).
